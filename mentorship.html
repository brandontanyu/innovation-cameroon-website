Innovation Cameroon Professional Developement Mentorship

Accelerate your growth with guidance from experienced professionals across corporate Settings

How It Works

Innovation Cameroon offers a dynamic mentorship program to help young graduates build their professional brand and confidently navigate the job market for jobs and internships. Delivered through interactive online sessions on Google Meet, it provides expert guidance on career readiness and networking.

Facilitators also benefit by enhancing their leadership, teaching practice, and professional visibility.

Program Timeline: Starts October 17, 2025 and ends November 15,2025
