Mentorship Program

Accelerate your growth with guidance from experienced professionals across corporate Settings

How It Works

This is a structured program focused on personal branding and global career readiness for engineering students. Delivered via online webinars, workshops, demos, coaching, and peer review. Some of the major modules include - Global‑Standard CV & Cover Letter, Personal Pitch & Storytelling, Building an Online Portfolio. Each module will take a maximum of 2 hours.

This Mentorship session will use Google Meet for reliability, three (3) sessions per week over a period of five (5) weeks with 25 - 30 participants

Mentors benefit through professional branding, leadership & teaching practice, increased visibility, scholarship impact, portfolio/case documentation, career advancement signals, and lifelong networks with successful mentees.
